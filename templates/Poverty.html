What’s the goal here?

Aims to end poverty in all its forms and ensuring that all have access to resources and economic opportunities necessary to lead a fulfilling life.

How can we achieve no poverty?

To achieve no poverty, we must invest in education and skills training, creating opportunities for stable employment. We need to establish robust social safety nets, ensuring access to healthcare and basic services. By promoting gender equality and empowering marginalized communities, we can address root causes of poverty.

Why?

Economic Opportunities: Create sustainable economic growth by fostering industries. Social Safety Nets: Establish robust social safety net programs that provide financial support. Education and Skills: Ensure accessible, quality education and skills training. Sustainable Development: Implement environmentally conscious strategies that prioritize long-term prosperity without depleting resources.

What’s the goal here?

Ensuring that all people have access to resources, basic services, and economic opportunities.
